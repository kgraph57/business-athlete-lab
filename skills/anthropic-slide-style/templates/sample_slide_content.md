# Anthropic Slide Style Demo

---

## Design System Overview

**Visual Identity**

This presentation demonstrates the Anthropic slide design system. The style is characterized by warmth, clarity, and intellectual depth.

---

## Color Palette

**Three Core Colors**

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Cream | #FDFDFB | Background |
| Terracotta | #C87E6F | Primary accent, card backgrounds |
| Warm Brown | #A56A57 | Secondary accent |
| Beige | #F2E4D5 | Light card backgrounds |

---

## Typography

**Font Pairing**

Headings use **Playfair Display** (bold serif) for elegance and authority. Body text uses **Inter** (clean sans-serif) for readability.

---

## Slide Types

**Three Common Layouts**

- **Title Slide**: Large centered heading with a subtitle.
- **Content Slide**: Heading with bullet points or paragraphs.
- **Card Slide**: Three colored cards side-by-side for comparisons.

## Illustration Style

**Hand-Drawn Aesthetic**

Illustrations use simple, sketchy line art with minimal color fills. Icons are drawn in a loose, organic style that feels approachable and human.
